Below you will find a favorite scene of mine from one of my favorite movies

Doctor Sleep

I love this scene because it is in one of my favorite movies of all time, Doctor Sleep. This movie is a sequel to The Shining. The reason I like this scene is because it shows how powerful someone with the "shine" can be and how you can set up traps within your mind. In this scene the lady is figuring out how to control her "shine" and stop the bad people from killing her and stealing her "shine".
